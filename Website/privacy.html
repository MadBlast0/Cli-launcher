Privacy Policy

This page explains what CLI Launcher's website stores in your browser, what public data it reads, and the choices and rights you have. It is written in plain language.

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

CLI Launcher is an open-source desktop application and website. The project is published under the MIT license at github.com/MadBlast0/Cli-launcher. This policy covers the website (this site). The desktop app is described briefly under "The desktop application" below.

What we store in your browser

We use a small number of first-party cookies and the equivalent localStorage values. These never leave your device and are not sent to any analytics or advertising service:

Name Category Purpose Duration
clla_consent Strictly necessary Remembers the cookie choices you made in the consent banner. Up to 1 year
clla_theme Preferences Remembers whether you prefer light or dark mode. Up to 1 year
theme (localStorage) Preferences Fallback mirror of your theme choice when cookies are unavailable. Until cleared

"Strictly necessary" cookies cannot be disabled — the site requires them to remember your consent and function. "Preferences" and "Analytics" are optional and only set after you accept them.

No tracking, no accounts

We do not set third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies, and this site has no user accounts or contact forms that collect personal information.

Public data we read

If you accept Analytics cookies, this site fetches public information from the GitHub API (such as the repository's star count) to display a live badge. This request is made from your browser directly to api.github.com and contains no personally identifiable information. If you reject Analytics, that fetch is skipped.

The desktop application

The CLI Launcher app runs locally on your computer. It installs and manages terminal AI agents (via npm, pip, or downloaded binaries) on your machine. The app does not transmit your code, prompts, or usage telemetry to us. Agent installations are performed by your own package managers.

Legal basis

Strictly necessary cookies are required for the site to function and to remember your consent. Preferences and analytics cookies are used only with your consent, which you grant through the banner on your first visit.

Your choices and rights

  • You can change or withdraw your cookie choices at any time via the Cookie settings link in the footer.
  • You can clear cookies and site data for this site from your browser's settings; the consent banner will reappear on your next visit.
  • You may request information about, or deletion of, any data you believe we hold by contacting us using the details below.

Data retention

Consent and theme cookies are stored for up to one year from your last choice, after which they expire and the banner will ask you again. No server-side profile of you is retained.

Third-party services

The only external service this site contacts is the GitHub API, and only for public repository metadata when Analytics is enabled. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of GitHub; refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Children

This website is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be noted on the site.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised through the project's GitHub issues.
